Enjoy nature and sports in this magical place
where the forrest meets the city of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Brekke

Tour-orientering in Nordmarka

Orienteering is a group of sports that require navigational skills using a map and compass to navigate from point to point in terrain whiles walking.

Go for a swim in Brekkedammen

Brekkedammen by Brekke is a popular place to take a swim in fresh water, with the cleanest water in all of Oslo as it comes from Maridansvannet, Oslos drinking water source

Cycling Route "Ring 3"

On of the rountes starting at Brekke and going into Nordmarka, is so popular that it has been called "Ring 3", an extention of the highways around Oslo.

History of Brekke

The area is named after Brekke farm. The name Brekke was first mentioned in 1369. Aside from farming, Brekke is also known for the large saw and smelters that from the 1500 were use for producing iron.
